Privacy

The Tube app doesn't track anything.

No app analytics. No watch history. No click maps. No tiny spreadsheet where I rate your thumbnail decisions. Tube just opens streaming websites in a native Mac window and minds its business.

Your accounts, recommendations, ads, cookies, and video history are handled by each streaming provider inside WebKit. Tube does not collect credentials or run its own tracking layer on top.

This landing page uses Landingana to measure page views and link clicks. That analytics script does not run inside Tube and cannot access your streaming accounts, credentials, recommendations, or watch history.

If a provider needs your location for local or live programming, macOS asks for permission and WebKit shares it directly with that provider. Tube does not store your location.
